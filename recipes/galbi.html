Galbi

Description

LA Galbi (LA-style galbi) is a Korean dish made with short ribs that have been marinated in a sweet and savory sauce. It is a popular dish at Korean BBQ restaurants, and it can also be made at home.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds beef short ribs, cut flanken style (thinly sliced across the bone)
  • 1 Korean pear, grated
  • 1 onion, grated
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 tablespoons rice wine
  • 1 tablespoon grated garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  • In a large bowl, combine all of the ingredients. Mix well to coat the ribs in the marinade.
  • Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, or overnight.
  • When ready to cook, preheat a grill or grill pan over medium heat.
  • Grill the ribs for 2-3 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  • Serve immediately with your favorite Korean BBQ side dishes, such as rice, lettuce wraps, and kimchi.
