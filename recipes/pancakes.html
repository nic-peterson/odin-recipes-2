Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a flat cake prepared from a starch-based batter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan. They are often thin and round, but can also be made thick and fluffy. Pancakes are typically made with flour, milk, eggs, and baking powder, and can be sweetened or savory. Pancakes are a popular breakfast food in many cultures around the world. They are often served with syrup, butter, fruit, and whipped cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk together the milk, egg, and vegetable oil.
  3. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients and whisk until just combined. Do not overmix.
  4. Heat a lightly greased griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  5. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the hot griddle or frying pan for each pancake.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side, or until golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Serve immediately with your favorite toppings, such as syrup, butter, fruit, or whipped cream.
